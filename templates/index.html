Our Mission: Demistify healther living and motivate actionable health behavior change through data science!


Two Simple Steps to Health Awareness:

1. Take a brief survey about your demographics, health behaviors and quality of life.

2. Receive a personalized Health Risk and Resiliency Profile that...

-Predicts your current risk for developing certain chronic illnesses (Diabetes)

-Illustrates how changing your health behaviors can impact your risk

-Provides feasible suggestions for reducing your risk -- your RX for a Healthier You

-Gives an overview of your general health behaviors -- your Health Behavior Archetype
